How to play

To start the game, click the "New Word" button.
Looking at the game page below, there are two lines of text, the blue one
is the definition and the purple one is the first two letters of the word. Read
the definition and complete the word by pressing the buttons of the keyboard
on the screen.

When you get the word right, a message will pop up, displaying your score and
the high score of the previous guest.
To get your next word, click "New Word" again.

What ECHO stands for

ECHO stands for Educational Cognitive Healing Octopus. Our mascot
is a purple octopus named Harry, who can be seen throughout the website.
We hope to help students, or anyone, who struggle with spelling in a fun way.

Our goals

Here at ECHO we strive to help students with spelling and memorization.
ECHO is directed towards young adults or older children as the vocabulary
may be hard for younger children.

The colours of ECHO are blue and purple. Blue represents calmness and
comfort, perfect for a learning environment. Purple represents wisdom
and transformation, fitting for the educational aspect of the game.
